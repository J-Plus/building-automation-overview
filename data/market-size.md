## Growth Drivers
1. IoT, AI, and cloud-based analytics integration
2. Government smart city investments and green building initiatives
3. Energy efficiency regulations and sustainability mandates
4. Growing need to enhance occupant comfort, productivity, and security
5. Rising demand for data-driven decision making
6. Environmental sustainability mandates

## Key Challenges
1. System customization difficulties
2. **Shortage of skilled professionals**
3. Lack of standardized communication protocols
4. Cybersecurity and data privacy concerns
